Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Fairo (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) operates and explains our commitment to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Fairo does not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • User accounts or login information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

2. Internet Usage & Language Detection

Fairo may use an internet connection temporarily to detect the user’s country or system locale in order to display the app in the most appropriate language.

Important clarifications:

  • No country or locale information is stored
  • No identifiable data is logged or transmitted
  • This detection occurs only at runtime
  • The information is not linked to any individual user

If no internet connection is available, the app continues to function normally using default language settings.

3. Data Storage

All app data (such as saved wheels or options) is stored locally on the user’s device only.

  • No data is uploaded to external servers
  • No cloud storage or synchronization is used

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

Fairo does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

If third-party services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Children’s Privacy

Fairo does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected, the app is safe for general audiences.

6. Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of personal data exposure through the app.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
